Delete Your Cockro Account

Cockro allows you to sign in with your Google account and create a profile (name, username, profile picture) and share posts, photos, comments, and join communities.

If you want to delete your Cockro account and associated data, you can request deletion at any time.

How to request account deletion

Please send an email from the Google account that you use to sign in to Cockro to the following address:

Email: _fitnessakash08@gmail.com

In your email, include:

  • Your Google account email address used in the app
  • Your Cockro username (if you set one)
  • A clear request to delete your Cockro account and all associated data

What data will be deleted

When we process your deletion request, we will delete:

  • Your account profile (name, username, profile picture)
  • Your posts and uploaded photos
  • Your comments
  • Your community memberships and related activity

We may keep limited data only if we are legally required or have a legitimate security reason (for example, to prevent abuse or fraud).

How long does it take?

We aim to delete your account and associated data within 30 days after we receive your email request.
